About Move Express

Smarter Way to Move Your World

MoveExpress is an online global goods transportation service booking platform that allows you to safely and quickly deliver your shipments anywhere in the world. With our state-of-the-art technology, you can track your cargo in real time, get live updates and stay informed throughout the entire delivery process. With a professional team of experienced logistics experts and over 400 logistics companies on our platform,

Real Time Tracking & Professional Team We understand how important it is for customers to monitor their shipment’s progress and receive accurate information about its location at all times – this is why we have enabled real time tracking across all orders placed on MoveExpress so as long as your device has internet connectivity; you will be able to follow every step taken by your shipper. On top of that if any complications arise during transit our expert team will be there 12/7 ready to assist – whether it’s getting through customs or locating lost packages; no job is too small or large for them!
